About VendPlug

Our mission is to digitize and organize Africa’s informal market system. We empower local economies by simplifying how people shop, pay, and connect through secure digital experiences.

Beyond shopping with ease, safety, and comfort — every order placed on VendPlug helps provide employment and economic empowerment to local agents within our communities.

A dedicated agent is out in the market, shopping with your submitted list — ensuring you get the right items, fresh and on time!

This customer is all smiles — she just received her full order and can now spend quality time with her loved ones, hassle-free.

You can pick up sorted and packed orders from VendPlug pickup stations — making it easy to collect everything on your way home from work. No more going round the market in traffic or stress.

Escrow for Safer Marketplaces

In many online marketplaces, transactions happen directly between buyers and sellers—with little protection if things go wrong. VendPlug changes that by adding a secure escrow layer to the buying experience.

Frequently Asked Questions

🔐 What is VendPlug Escrow?

VendPlug Escrow is a secure payment solution that holds funds until a buyer confirms they have received the product as described. You can buy from anyone, anywhere in Nigeria, knowing your money is safe.

📱 How do I join VendPlug as a vendor or agent?

You can register interest through our platform and our team will follow up with onboarding instructions.

🧾 What can I buy with VendPlug?

Everything you’d find in a real market! From tomatoes, rice, onions, palm oil, beans, peppers, meat, fish, and yams — to electronics, fabrics, phone accessories, shoes, bags, clothes, and even services like tailoring or repairs. If it's in a market, you can get it through VendPlug.
