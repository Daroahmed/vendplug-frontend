Payouts & Transparency

Trust is at the core of VendPlug. Every transaction is transparent, from the moment a buyer pays to the moment a vendor gets paid.

1. Buyer Pays Securely

Funds are held safely in escrow as soon as an order is placed.

2. Order is Fulfilled

The vendor and/or agent delivers the product, knowing the payment is guaranteed.

3. Buyer Confirms Receipt

The buyer confirms they have received the order, triggering the next step.

4. Payout is Released

The funds are automatically and instantly released from escrow to the vendor and agent.
